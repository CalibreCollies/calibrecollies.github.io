Makarois places at the 2009 Collie Club of Amercia National Specialty
 Makarois, Calibre's Self-Fulfilling Phrophecy placed fourth in the 12 - 18 month puppy dog class at the 2009 Collie Club of America Specialty in West Springfield, MA under judge Judy Bryant.
 
 Makarois is sired by CH Kimberee Under the Influence and is out of CH Calibre's Unforgettable, a CH Bethany's Preacher Man daughter.
